1. Who we are

OCTAVIRE B.V. ("OCTAVIRE", "we", "us") is a company registered in the Netherlands with its principal office in Rotterdam. For EU users, we act as Data Controller for account data and as Data Processor for content uploaded through the product.

2. What we collect

Account data

  • Name, email address, password hash, profile picture
  • Billing information (processed by Stripe; we never see raw card numbers)
  • Studio details (if applicable): business name, VAT number, country

Content you upload

  • Design files, stencils, and portfolio imagery
  • Body-scan data (LiDAR meshes and optional photos)
  • Client bookings, deposits, and messages routed through OCTAVIRE

Usage telemetry

  • Anonymous feature usage (only with explicit consent)
  • Crash and performance logs, stripped of identifying content
  • Device type, OS version, app version

3. Why we collect it

We use your data strictly to: provide the product, process payments, support your account, investigate abuse, and — only with explicit consent — improve the product. We do not sell data. We do not serve advertising. We do not profile users across third-party websites.

4. Body scans — special care

Body-scan data is processed on-device by default. Cloud sync is opt-in per scan. Scans are:

  • Encrypted with a per-user key derived from your password
  • Stored in EU regions (Amsterdam / Dublin)
  • Never used to train foundation models
  • Only used to train your own style model, and only if you enable it

5. Sharing & third parties

We use a small number of carefully vetted sub-processors. Each is bound by a DPA and SCCs where applicable.

  • Stripe — payment processing (US/EU, PCI-DSS Level 1)
  • AWS — hosting & storage (EU regions by default)
  • Cloudflare — DDoS protection & CDN
  • Postmark — transactional email
  • PostHog — product analytics (self-hosted, EU)

A current list is available at privacy@octavire.com.

6. Retention

Account data is retained while your account is active. On deletion, account data is removed within 30 days across all production systems and backups. Invoices are retained for 10 years as required by Dutch tax law.

7. Your rights

Under GDPR, CCPA, and UK-GDPR you have the right to: access your data, rectify it, erase it, restrict its processing, port it to another provider, object to processing, and lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority (in NL: Autoriteit Persoonsgegevens).

Exercise any right in-app: Settings → Privacy → My Data — or email privacy@octavire.com. We respond within 30 days.

8. Security

TLS 1.3 in transit. AES-256 at rest. Production access is MFA-gated, audit-logged, and restricted to a named engineering team. Full detail on our Security page.

9. Children

OCTAVIRE is not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has created an account, contact us and we will delete it.

10. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we'll notify account holders by email at least 30 days before the change takes effect. The "Effective" date above is always the current version.
