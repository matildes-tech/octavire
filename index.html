From first scan to final line — one intelligent canvas that cuts prep time by 10× without cutting corners.

01

Scan any body in seconds.

Proprietary, patented 3D capture turns a phone camera sweep into a millimetre-accurate model of any body part. No rigs. No markers. Just the body, truly.

02

Designs that fit the body.

Our morphology engine adapts every stroke to the real curvature, volume, and muscle contour of your client's anatomy — so a rose wraps a shoulder, not fights it.

Collaborate without interruption.

Share, iterate, and approve with clients and collaborating artists — all in one live canvas. No email-thread graveyard. No lost versions.

04

Smart stencils that learn your line.

A generative algorithm studies your hand, your pressure, your flow. Then it renders print-ready stencils in your style — not its. The more you draw, the more it sounds like you.

Real-dimension. Print once.

Every stencil is calibrated to the client's actual body measurements, so the first print is the final print. No tape, no retries, no wasted transfer paper.

06

Prompt it like a creative director.

Voice and text commands handle layout, composition, line weight, style — the whole vocabulary of the craft, spoken naturally. "Tighter line, more negative space, rotate 6°."

07

Gloves stay on.

Hand-gesture control lets you rotate, zoom, place, and confirm — without breaking sterile field or workflow. No styluses dropped. No tablets contaminated.

08

Clients scan themselves.

Clients capture their own body and preview any design on their skin before booking. More confident decisions, fewer last-minute cancellations, zero surprises.

09

See it in five, twenty, fifty years.

Our aging engine simulates skin elasticity, weight change, and ink migration — so your client commits to the long-term version of the piece, not just the fresh one.

Terms & Conditions

By using OCTAVIRE you agree to our service terms. Accounts are personal, non-transferable, and must be used in compliance with local tattoo licensing laws. We reserve the right to suspend accounts engaged in prohibited activities (impersonation, copyright infringement, harassment). Beta access is granted at our discretion and may change before general availability.

Last updated: April 2026

Privacy Policy

We collect only what's needed to run the product: account info, portfolio content you upload, body-scan data you explicitly capture, and usage telemetry. Body scans are processed on-device by default. Nothing is sold to third parties. You can export or delete all your data at any time.

Last updated: April 2026

Cookie Policy

We use strictly-necessary cookies (session, security, language) and — with your consent — anonymous analytics to improve the product. No advertising cookies, ever. You can change your preferences any time from the cookie banner or account settings.

Last updated: April 2026

Refund Policy

Pro subscriptions include a 14-day trial — cancel any time before it ends and you won't be billed. After billing, we offer full refunds within 30 days, no questions asked, for monthly subscriptions. Annual plans are pro-rated. Enterprise refunds follow the terms of your MSA.

Last updated: April 2026

GDPR Compliance

OCTAVIRE acts as Data Controller for its customers' account data and Data Processor for content uploaded through the product. We support all GDPR rights (access, rectification, erasure, portability, objection) and sign a Data Processing Agreement with enterprise customers on request. Our EU operations are based in Rotterdam, NL; data is stored in EU regions by default.

Last updated: April 2026

Data Deletion Request

You can delete your account and all associated data directly from Settings → Account → Delete, or by emailing privacy@octavire.com. Deletion is irreversible and completes within 30 days across all systems and backups. A signed confirmation is emailed once complete.
