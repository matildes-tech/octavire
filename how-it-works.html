How it works

From scan to skin in three steps.

The studio software that turns a body scan, an AI sketch and an AR preview into a confirmed booking. No new hardware. No new workflow to learn.

01

Scan

Capture the client's body in 3D in under ten seconds. The iPad or iPhone you already own becomes a millimetre-accurate scanner. No markers. No calibration. No external rig.

  • Full body mesh in < 10 seconds
  • Works on any skin tone or body type
  • On-device, encrypted by default
  • Client can scan from home via the free companion app
02

Design

Sketch fast in a canvas built for tattoo artists. Run AI variations on style, scale and position without leaving the app. The tool removes the staring-at-blank-canvas part. You still draw.

  • Pressure-true brush engine
  • Style-aware AI variations (fine-line, blackwork, dotwork, traditional)
  • Vector + raster on one canvas
  • Smart line-cleanup, one-tap symmetry across limbs
03

Place

Project the design onto the client's actual 3D body in AR. Re-mesh to the curve of the limb. Approve the placement before the needle touches skin. Export a true-scale stencil ready to print.

  • Live AR preview on the client
  • Curve-aware re-meshing
  • True-scale stencil export, ready to print
  • Client signs off remotely or in-studio
